I like maths, coding, pubs, travel, basketball, guitar, singing, chess and most recently, cycling. I build things that I think are interesting and I like to talk about it. I’m always up for a chat over a coffee or beer - just get in touch.

Somewhere in the middle of that I worked as a Software Engineer at Nested and Scott Logic.
