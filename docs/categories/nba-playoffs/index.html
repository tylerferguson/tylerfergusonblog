What if Round 1 of the NBA Playoffs was best of 5?

Prior to the 2002–03 NBA season the first round of the NBA Playoffs was a best of 5 format. It had been this way since the 1984 season (a year that’s been coming up an awful lot lately with a certain documentary coming out). Before 1984 the league had experimented with a few different formats. But the post-1984 format is very familiar to us all: 4 rounds in the playoffs, each round is a best of 7 series.
